About us

Alp Music Academy is one of the very few music training centres with affiliation and certification with London school of Music and Trinity School London. It boasts of musical training for most instruments like Guitar, Drums, Keyboard, Saxophone and Violin to name a few.
Started in 2017, the academy aims to attend to the untapped potential in most work spaces and create niche pool of musicians who could afford to try their hand at it and cope up with the carefully designed syllabus that takes just 4 months to become a musician. Training specially designed for working class people with 4 months to get them from a beginner to basic player, basic player to an intermediate level and intermediate player to advanced level.
